I am passionate about solving problems with code. My approach is to write simple, easy-to-understand code that utilizes popular Python packages, rather than focusing on optimization or specialized libraries. I've developed several scripts for analyzing the results of Molecular Dynamics and Monte Carlo simulations, with a focus on creating accessible and straightforward solutions.

For highly optimized Molecular Dynamics analysis tools, you can explore popular packages like freud and MDAnalysis .
