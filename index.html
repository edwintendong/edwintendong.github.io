Hi there! Welcome to my page.
I am a passionate researcher working in the fields of computational softmatter Physics, complex systems and climate science. My research focuses on understanding the fundamental properties and dynamics at nanoscale of complex materials such as nano-confined fluids, colloids, ionic liquids, metal organic framworks, proteins and biomolecules etc. using the equilibrium and nonequilibrium Molecular Dynamics, Density Functional Theory and Machine Learning. I also work on understanding extreme event in climate based on analysis of sattelite data using Machine learning techniques.
I currently work as a Tutor/Researcher in Soft Condensed Matter Physics and Climate Sciences at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) - Cameroon.
